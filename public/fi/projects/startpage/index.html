Startpage on Yhdysvalloissa ja Alankomaissa ylläpidetty SaaS joka tarjoaa anonyymit Googlehaut ja kuvat ilmaisen väliyspalvelimen kautta. Its sibling project ixquick.eu, however, offer results from other search engine.

Ixquick also provides the stand-alone Ixquick Proxy service that allows users to open search results via proxy.

As of February 19, 2015, Startpage.com is the default search engine in the Tor Browser.
